About the Project: IllnessInsight

Welcome to IllnessInsight, an innovative, AI-powered platform designed to assist users in predicting the likelihood of various diseases based on their health parameters. This project leverages advanced machine learning models and cutting-edge generative AI technology to provide accurate predictions and personalized assistance.

Key Features:

  • ✅ Disease Prediction: Analyze critical health metrics to predict the risk of Heart Disease, Diabetes, Cancer, and COVID-19.
  • ✅ AI-Powered Chat Assistance: Powered by Google's Gemini AI, this feature acts as your virtual healthcare assistant.
  • ✅ Nearby Hospital Finder: Instantly locate hospitals and clinics near you using your device's location.
